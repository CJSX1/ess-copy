Vaidehi Ordering

Detailed steps about pre-ordering at IITB (from Vaidehi Buyers Market and others) using the Lokacart Application ( android and IoS ) will be hereafter always updated here.

Vaidehi Buyers Market, a visiting vegetable and fruit vendor at IIT during the period of the lockdown, is now ready to get orders for their products via Lokacart (an app built by IIT Bombay in association with its alumni).

Vaidehi Buyers Market (VBM) sources fresh rural (gavaraan) fruits and vegetables directly from their farms around village Vaishnawdham, Junnar, Pune. Vaidehi Market’s products are available in 1kg , half kg units. They also make available pre-packaged homemade products.

Following are some more notes:

Please note improvisations (1) to (4) for delivery on Wednesday, based on experiences last Saturday
  1. PRE-ORDERS BY PREVIOUS DAY 4 PM: The pre-orders will need to be made through the Lokacart App at least 18 hours prior to the closest delivery day (Wednesday or Saturday) to avoid delays. Thus, for delivery on Wednesday noon time, request you to place the order latest by 4 PM on Tuesday. All orders placed after 4 PM on Tuesday shall be delivered during the next slot i.e. Saturday.
  2. CANCELLATION POSSIBLE UNTIL PREVIOUS DAY 4 PM: Please note that orders that have been placed are “processed” by VBM only after 4 PM on Tuesday . Therefore if you wish to cancel your order, you will have to do it before 4 PM. Orders that have already been processed by VBM cannot be cancelled.
  3. BILL FAITHFUL TO DELIVERY: Hereafter, the seller will try and ensure that products on display are available during delivery. However, in the rare event of a product becoming unavailable, your final bill that will be generated will have only those products that you have ordered and are sure to receive at the time of delivery. Therefore, please check the bill that is generated in the Lokacart App to cross check.
  4. SINGLE LOCATION FOR DELIVERY starting at 12:30 PM: Given the spacious and covered nature of the Octagon near the library at HILLSIDE, Delivery will start at 12:30 PM at the Octagon near the library at HILLSIDE. The specific timing of the delivery will also be communicated via the Lokacart App app+sms. Your E token will be generated only after packing and arrangements have been done so that delay is minimized.
  5. PRODUCT IMAGES: Based on the user feedback, an appropriate image has been displayed by VBM for each product in the app.
  6. PLASTIC & CARTON-FREE DELIVERY: roducts pre-ordered through the app will be pre-packed in CRATES (see image) with name and contact number of customers) and its contents handed over to the customer in the customer’s own bag during their next delivery which is either Wednesday or Saturday.
  7. CONTACT REDUCTION: Needless to say, by ordering via the app, one would reduce contact time with other people thereby checking the possible spread of COVID 19.

Recall that if you do not have the Lokacart application installed OR are not registered on Lokacart:

  • Install Lokacart Andriod Application ( android and IoS available)
  • Select-: I don't have Lokacart account
  • For “Do you have seller number?” select "Yes"
  • Enter your phone number and seller phone number: 7264992919 (which is Vaidehi Buyers Market’s number)*.
  • You will receive a link via SMS for approval of the addition of the seller organization. Click on that link (to accept the seller)
  • The link will take you back to the app. Now in the app, select the organization (from which you want to make your order)

If you already have the Lokacart application installed AND are already registered on Lokacart:

  • Go to “Referrals” and select “I have a seller number”
  • Enter seller number: 7264992919 (which is Vaidehi Buyers Market number)*.
  • You will receive a link via SMS for approval of the addition of the seller organization. Click on that link (to accept the seller)
  • The link will take you back to the app. Now in the app, select the organization (from which you want to make your order)
