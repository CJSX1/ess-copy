Chhadva Ordering

Detailed steps about pre-ordering at IITB from Chhadva using the Lokacart Application ( android and IoS ) will be hereafter always updated here.

  • For those who would like to instead Whatsapp their orders to Chhadva, could do so at 8080558558 (while specifying their email ids) as per the schedule mentioned in the table to follow.
  • Collection time/date will be emailed to the email id (specified through Lokacart/Whatsapp) - hence please ensure you specify the correct email id.
Chhadva Foods & Grains has a large number of its regularly ordered products, along with price and some other details updated on Lokacart and will have them ready for ordering by Tuesday 9th June 2020. The deliveries under the new scheme will begin on Thursday, 11th June.

Following are some more notes:

  1. Delivery Starting At 4 Pm At Octagon Next To Central Library On Tuesdays, Wednesdays, Thursdays, Fridays And Saturdays.
  2. PRE-ORDERS 1 DAY BEFORE DELIVERY: The pre-orders will need to be made through the Lokacart App at least 24 hours prior to the closest delivery day. Chhadva will adhere to the following schedule
    Tentative timeline for Chhadva orders Tentative day of Delivery
    Until 12 noon on Monday Tuesday
    Until 12 noon on Tuesday Wednesday
    Until 12 noon on Wednesday Thursday
    Until 12 noon on Thursday Friday
    Until 12 noon on Friday Saturday
    All orders placed after 12 noon will be delivered during the next slot (or earlier).
  3. CANCELLATION POSSIBLE UNTIL CHHADVA PROCESSES YOUR ORDER: If you wish to cancel your order (perhaps to re-order), you will have to do it before Chhadva processes your order. You can check on your Lokacart app whether your or not Chhadva has processed your order. You can cancel only if Chhadva has not yet processed your order.
  4. ITEMS MISSING FROM THE PRODUCT LIST: If you think Chhadva generally sells an item missing from its current product catalog on Lokacart, and you desire to purchase those items, you can mention the names and quantity of those items in the “Comments” section while checking out of the purchase. In fact, the comments section has the following watermark: “Missing products can be mentioned here”. Please see the image beside this description.
  5. BILL FAITHFUL TO DELIVERY: Before you place your orders, Chhadva will try and ensure that products on display are available during delivery. However, in the rare event of a product becoming unavailable, your final bill (in hard copy) that will be generated will have only those products that you have ordered (include those in the Comment section) and are sure to receive at the time of delivery. Therefore, please check the hard copy bill at the time of delivery to cross check.
  6. SINGLE LOCATION FOR DELIVERY starting at 4 PM: Given the spacious and covered nature of the Octagon near the library at HILLSIDE, Delivery will start at 4 PM at the Octagon near the library at HILLSIDE. The specific timing of the delivery will also be communicated by Chhada either directly or via the Lokacart App +sms.
  7. CONTACT REDUCTION: Needless to say, by ordering via the app and adhering to the time specified for the delivery, one would reduce contact time with other people thereby checking the possible spread of COVID 19.

If you do not have the Lokacart application installed OR not registered on Lokacart:

  • Install Lokacart Andriod Application ( android and IoS available)
  • Select-: I don't have Lokacart account
  • For “Do you have seller number?” select "Yes"
  • Enter your phone number and seller phone number: 8080558558 (which is Chhadva Foods and Grain’s number)*.
  • You will receive a link via SMS for approval of the addition of the seller organization. Click on that link (to accept the seller)
  • The link will take you back to the app. Now in the app, select the organization (from which you want to make your order)

If you already have the Lokacart application installed AND are already registered on Lokacart:

  • Go to “Referrals” and select “I have a seller number”
  • Enter seller number: 8080558558 (which is Chhadva Foods and Grain’s number)*.
  • You will receive a link via SMS for approval of the addition of the seller organization. Click on that link (to accept the seller)
  • The link will take you back to the app. Now in the app, select the organization (from which you want to make your order)
