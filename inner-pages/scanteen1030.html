Staff Canteen Vegetable Market

Staff Canteen Vegetable Market (Sunday Igatpuri Farmers Market) Opens at 10:30 a.m.

  • Click to issue E-tokens. (use your NON-IITB email id for getting the token) – Time 9:30 a.m.
  • You can check the live status of the token being served at (Here)

Instructions

  1. Should reach the staff canteen only through infinity corridor. You can park your vehicles either in front of the main building or adjacent to the library. Residents will not be allowed to enter through the backside gate to the staff canteen and park vehicles close to the staff canteen.
  2. Reach the venue only when the token being served is 5 numbers ahead of your token number. That means, if your token number is 25, please come when the status of the token served is 20. Do not come early. Those who come early will be asked to go back from the location to avoid crowding. Since we are going to maintain less number people inside the market, the movement of tokens may be slower.
  3. Wear masks and maintain social distancing among your selves and with the vendor in the market.
  4. Only one person will be allowed per token into the vegetable market.
  5. Please maintain physical distancing with volunteers as well. We will not issue any token at the venue.

PLEASE COOPERATE WITH VOLUNTEERS FOR HEALTH & SAFETY OF ALL CAMPUS RESIDENTS.
